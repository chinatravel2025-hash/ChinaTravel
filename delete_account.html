How to Delete Your Account

App Name: ChinaTravel

Developer: Shanghai Chunhuo Information Technology Co., Ltd.

Last Updated: December 2025

If you no longer wish to use the ChinaTravel app and want to delete your account, you can do so directly within the app. This page explains the steps to delete your account and what happens to your data.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the ChinaTravel app on your device.
  2. Navigate to "Preferences" (Settings) from the main menu.
  3. Select "Close account" option.
  4. Enter your user nickname in the input field that appears.
  5. Review the warning message that explains all data will be cleared and cannot be recovered.
  6. Tap "Close" to confirm the account deletion, or tap "Cancel" if you change your mind.
  7. After confirmation, your account will be permanently deleted and you will be logged out automatically.

Data Deletion and Retention

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered:

Data Type Description
User Account Information Your email address, nickname, profile information, and account credentials
Travel Customization Data All your customized travel itineraries, preferences, and travel plans
Order Records All booking records, order history, and transaction information
Favorite Data Your saved favorite cities, attractions, restaurants, and shops
User Content Any travel experience sharing, comments, or feedback you have posted
Chat History All conversation records with customer service or other users
Personal Preferences App settings, notification preferences, and other personalized configurations

⚠️ Important Warning: All data will be cleared and cannot be recovered after closing your account. Please make sure you have backed up any important information before proceeding with account deletion.

Data Retention Period

After you submit the account deletion request:

  • Immediate Deletion: Your account and most associated data will be deleted immediately upon successful account closure.
  • No Retention Period: We do not retain any of your personal data after account deletion for any additional period, except as required by applicable laws and regulations.
  • Legal Requirements: In certain limited circumstances, we may be required by law to retain certain information (such as transaction records) for a specific period. Such data will be stored securely and will not be used for any other purposes.

What Happens After Account Deletion

  • You will be automatically logged out from the app.
  • You will be redirected to the login page.
  • All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
  • You will not be able to recover your account or any associated data.
  • If you wish to use the app again in the future, you will need to create a new account.
