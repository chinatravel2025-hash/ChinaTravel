# Google Play 应用主要目的声明

## What is the main purpose of your app?

### 英文版本：

**ChinaTravel is a comprehensive travel planning application designed to help users discover, explore, and plan their trips to China. The app provides detailed information about cities, attractions, restaurants, and travel itineraries, along with interactive maps to visualize destinations. Users can browse travel products, view sightseeing details, and access customized travel services through an integrated chat feature with travel guides. The app aims to enhance the travel planning experience by offering visual context, geographical information, and personalized travel recommendations.**

### 中文版本：

**ChinaTravel是一个综合性的旅游规划应用，旨在帮助用户发现、探索和规划中国之旅。该应用提供城市、景点、餐厅和旅行行程的详细信息，以及用于可视化目的地的交互式地图。用户可以通过与导游的集成聊天功能浏览旅游产品、查看景点详情并访问定制化旅游服务。该应用旨在通过提供视觉上下文、地理信息和个性化旅游推荐来增强旅行规划体验。**

## 核心功能点

1. **旅游目的地探索** - 浏览城市、景点信息
2. **行程规划** - 查看和规划旅行路线
3. **交互式地图** - 可视化景点位置
4. **定制化服务** - 通过聊天功能获取个性化旅游建议
5. **旅游产品** - 浏览和预订旅行产品
