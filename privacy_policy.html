Shanghai Chunhuo Information Technology Co., Ltd. ("we", "us" or "our") attaches great importance to the protection of your personal information and privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the ChinaTravel App (the "App"), as well as your rights regarding your personal information. By downloading, installing, and using this App, you are deemed to have fully read, understood, and agreed to the entire content of this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope of Application

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected, used, and processed by us when you use the ChinaTravel App, including the information you provide when using the App's customized travel services, and the information automatically collected during your use of the App.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to the following information:

  1. Keyword information you enter when using the App's scenic spot search function;
  2. Public information you voluntarily publish in the App, such as travel experience sharing and scenic spot reviews;
  3. Information collected by third-party platforms linked to the App. Such third parties shall have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their privacy protection practices.

2. Collection and Use of Personal Information

We adhere to the principles of legality, necessity, and good faith in collecting your personal information, and will not collect irrelevant personal information without reason.

2.1 Information You Voluntarily Provide

  1. Basic Registration and Service Information: When you use the App's customized travel service, you may need to provide basic information such as your name, contact number, and email address. This information is used to communicate with you about travel plans, confirm reservations, and provide after-sales services.
  2. Travel Customization Information: To provide personalized China travel customization services, you may voluntarily provide information such as your travel time, number of travelers, travel budget, preferred scenic spots, dietary taboos, and special travel needs. This information will be used exclusively to tailor travel itineraries that meet your requirements.
  3. Payment-related Information: When booking travel products or services through the App, you may need to provide payment account information as required. Please note that we will never store your complete payment card details. Such information will be processed by our cooperating third-party payment service providers that comply with relevant financial security standards.

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you use the App, we may automatically collect relevant device and usage information to ensure the stable operation of the App and optimize your user experience, including but not limited to your device model, operating system version, App version, IP address, and usage records (such as viewed scenic spot pages and customized service access time).

2.3 Collection and Use of Permission-related Information

To provide you with high-quality customized travel services, the App will apply for relevant device permissions. We will only collect and use information through these permissions when you authorize them, and will not use the permissions for irrelevant purposes.

Permission Type Purpose of Use
Camera Permission When you need to take photos of travel-related materials (such as identity documents required for booking tickets, or scenery that you want to share with our customer service for customized reference), the App will use the camera function with your permission. We will only collect image information when you actively take photos.
Voice Permission This permission is used to facilitate your voice input when describing your travel needs, or voice communication with our customer service through the App. We will only collect your voice information during your active voice interaction.
Photo Permission This permission allows you to upload photos stored on your device (such as ID photos, preferred travel scenery photos) to the App for travel customization and reservation verification. We will not access or browse your photo album without authorization.

3. Sharing, Transfer and Disclosure of Personal Information

3.1 Sharing

We will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any irrelevant third parties without your prior consent, except in the following circumstances:

  1. We may share necessary personal information with our cooperating third-party payment service providers to complete payment processing. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it to provide payment services for us.
  2. When cooperating with travel service providers (such as hotels, scenic spot ticket offices) to fulfill your customized travel itinerary, we may share the minimum necessary personal information (such as your name and contact number) with them, and require them to strictly comply with privacy protection regulations.
  3. Sharing is required in accordance with laws and regulations, legal judicial decisions, or mandatory requirements of administrative authorities.
  4. Payment-related information: When you book travel products or services through the App, you may need to provide payment account information. Please note that we will not store your complete payment card details: Such information will be processed by the third-party payment service providers we cooperate with, who must comply with relevant financial security standards.

3.2 Transfer

We will not transfer your personal information to any third party unless:

  1. We have obtained your explicit prior consent;
  2. The transfer of personal information is involved in the process of merger, acquisition, or dissolution of the company. In such cases, we will inform you of the receiving party and require the receiving party to continue to assume the obligation of protecting your personal information.

3.3 Disclosure

We will only disclose your personal information in the following circumstances:

  1. With your explicit consent;
  2. Disclosure is required to comply with applicable laws and regulations, respond to judicial orders, or meet the requirements of government departments;
  3. Disclosure is necessary to protect our legitimate rights and interests, the safety of users, or public interests, and it is in line with relevant legal provisions.

4. Storage of Personal Information

  1. Storage Location: We will store the collected personal information on secure servers located within the territory of China, in compliance with relevant Chinese laws and regulations on data storage.
  2. Storage Period: We will only store your personal information for the period necessary to achieve the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy and in accordance with legal requirements. After the storage period expires, we will take secure measures such as deletion or anonymization of your personal information to ensure that it will no longer be used for business purposes.

5. Protection of Personal Information Security

We have adopted a series of technical and management security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, or loss:

  1. Technically, we use encryption technology to protect sensitive information, and set up firewalls and other technical barriers to prevent network attacks;
  2. Managerially, we formulate strict data security management systems, limit the scope of personnel accessing personal information, and conduct regular privacy protection training for relevant personnel to enhance their awareness of information security;
  3. If a personal information security incident occurs, we will immediately launch an emergency plan to control the scope of the incident, and in accordance with legal requirements, promptly inform you of the relevant situation and disposal measures through announcements or other appropriate means.

6. Your Rights to Personal Information

You enjoy the following rights regarding your personal information stored in the App:

  1. Right of Access: You can view the personal information you have filled in the App, such as your registration information and customized travel records;
  2. Right of Correction: If you find that your personal information stored in the App is incorrect or incomplete, you can modify it through the App's relevant settings or contact customer service for correction;
  3. Right of Deletion: You can request us to delete your personal information if it is no longer needed for the stated purposes, or if you revoke your consent to the use of the information;
  4. Right to Revoke Consent: You can revoke the permissions you have granted (such as camera and voice permissions) through your device settings. Please note that revoking consent may affect your use of the relevant functions of the App, but will not affect the validity of the previous information processing based on your consent.

To exercise the above rights, you can contact our customer service through the email chinatravel2025@163.com. We will verify your identity within a reasonable time and respond to your request in accordance with relevant laws and regulations.

When we make major revisions to the Privacy Policy, we will prominently remind you (such as popping up a notification in the App) and explain the content of the revisions.
